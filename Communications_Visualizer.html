Welcome to Communications Visualizer

Use this quick guide the first time you open the tool. It explains what each section does and how to explore the plots.

Line coding

Compare NRZ, RZ, Manchester, and AMI in both the time and frequency domains. Changing bit rate changes bit time and bandwidth.

Modulation

Switch between ASK, FSK, PSK, and QAM to see how carrier frequency, symbol rate, and noise affect waveform shape and spectrum.

Graph controls

Use the mouse wheel to zoom. Click and drag left or right to pan. Turn on the measurement tool when you want cursor readouts.

Views

RF view shows the real passband spectrum, while baseband view shows the underlying signal behavior used for I/Q-style analysis.
